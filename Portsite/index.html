Fish tank simulation

This project was done to create fish tank simulation.
    Fish is an AI that swims into the food the player gives and avoids rocks. The player can feed them by clicking anywhere beside the rock. All sprites are hand-drawn.
The AI contains:
  • Seek Behavior
  • Flee Behavior
  • Coherence (Follow other AI)
  • Seperation (Avoid overlap with other AI)
  • Alignment (Go same way as nearby AI)

Randomness

This project was done to learn about creating RNG in games.
    The player clicks on big dirt in the middle of the screen to try to ding. Every time a player digs, there is a 50% chance to succeed and gain either dirt or minerals. There is a 30% chance of getting minerals. There are 3 kinds of minerals players can get which are Iron(85%), Gold(10%), and Diamond(5%).
    Due to the nature of the game being boring, an additional goal is added. Players need to mine 1000 Diamonds to save their friend who is in jail from a tax invasion. The player can convert dirt and minerals into its higher tiers in a 1:10 ratio. The player can keep playing the game even if they saved their friend.
Random function contains:
  • Marble Bag
  • Predetermination
  • Progressive
  • Fixed Rate Probability

FSM

This project was done to learn about finite state machine.
    The system is themed as 'Guard VS Gnome'. Guard is the AI with FSM. The user clicks anywhere on the screen to summon the gnome. Gnome will walk randomly around the screen. When a gnome steps on bone, It will make a sound to alert any guard nearby.
AI has stated as follows:
  • Idle (stay still, is switch between Patrol)
  • Patrol (walking around, is switched between Idle)
  • Investigate (Walk to bone when hear a sound)
  • Chase (Chase and capture nearby gnome)
